## 公司簡介 · About

久盛商品陳列架股份有限公司（英文名 JEOU-SHENG MERCHANDISE-FRAME CO., LTD.，品牌 **JOSAM**）成立於 **1990 年**，總部位於台灣彰化，目前由第二代家族經營、財務穩健。公司專注於金屬家具製造與智慧工廠自動化，提供零售與工業用陳列／倉儲架系統，並具備從雷射切割、機器人焊接到粉體烤漆的**一條龍自動化製程**。產品與服務遍及台灣、美國、日本，提供標準化產品與客製化 OEM／ODM 解決方案。

JOSAM (JEOU-SHENG MERCHANDISE-FRAME CO., LTD.) was founded in **1990** in Changhua, Taiwan, and is now a financially stable, second-generation family business. We specialize in metal furniture manufacturing and smart-factory automation, delivering retail and industrial shelving / storage systems through an **end-to-end automated process** — laser cutting, robotic welding, and powder coating. We serve retail chains and logistics operations across Taiwan, the US, and Japan with both standardized products and custom OEM/ODM solutions.
